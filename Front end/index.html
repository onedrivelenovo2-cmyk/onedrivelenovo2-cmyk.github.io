Welcome to planet Earth!

Planet Earth is the third planet from the Sun, a rocky world uniquely known for supporting life, featuring liquid water, a life-sustaining oxygen-rich atmosphere, and a dynamic surface shaped by tectonic plates and water. It's the fifth-largest planet, an "ocean world" with over 70% covered by water, and protected by a magnetic field and atmosphere that shield it from most solar radiation.
